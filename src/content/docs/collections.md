title: "Collections"
---


Collections are hand-curated lists of books. Think of them as playlists - you decide exactly what goes in, and in what order. They show up in the sidebar, and any collection can optionally sync to your Kobo device.

<img src="/images/collections/collections-sidebar.webp" alt="Collections section in the sidebar showing five collections with book counts" class="img-xs img-bordered" />

## Creating a collection

Click the **three-dot menu** next to the COLLECTIONS heading in the sidebar and choose **New Collection**.

<img src="/images/collections/collections-sidebar-menu.webp" alt="Collections sidebar menu showing New Collection and Reorder options" class="img-xs img-bordered" />

Give it a name and pick an icon - both are required. After saving, you land directly on the new (empty) collection.

## Adding books

There are two ways to add books to a collection:

**From a book's context menu** - right-click any book cover (or use the three-dot menu on a book card) and choose **Add to collection**. A sheet slides up showing all your collections. Tap any collection to add the book instantly. You can also create a new collection from the same sheet without leaving the flow.

**Select mode** - in any library or search view, click **SELECT**, check the books you want, then use the action bar that appears at the bottom to add them to a collection in bulk.

## Collection detail

<img src="/images/collections/collection-detail.webp" alt="Resident Evil Universe collection showing 11 books across novels and comics" class="img-lg img-bordered" />

The collection detail page shows all books in the order they were added. You can search within the collection, switch between grid and list views, and toggle series collapse. A **SELECT** mode lets you remove specific books in bulk.

## Editing a collection

Click the **pencil icon** on the collection detail page to open the Edit Collection dialog.

<img src="/images/collections/edit-collection.webp" alt="Edit Collection dialog showing name, icon picker, Sync to Kobo toggle, and Delete button" class="img-sm img-bordered" />

From here you can:

- **Rename** the collection
- **Change the icon**
- **Sync to Kobo** - toggle this on to push every book in this collection to your paired Kobo as a named shelf. Books removed from the collection are removed from the Kobo shelf on the next sync. See [Kobo Sync](/kobo) for device setup and eligibility requirements.
- **Delete** the collection - clicking Delete once shows a "Confirm?" prompt before anything is erased. Deleting a collection never deletes the underlying books.

## Reordering collections

Click the **three-dot menu** next to COLLECTIONS and choose **Reorder**. Drag the collections into whatever order you want. On touch, use the up/down arrows.
